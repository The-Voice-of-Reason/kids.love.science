ABOUT THE PROJECT

"The Voice of Reason" is a global community of individuals who are passionate about children′s education, especially in the field of science and exploration. Our content is designed to stimulate children′s thirst for knowledge and encourage creativity through age-appropriate immersive stories, allowing kids to realize their full potential.

WE NEED YOUR HELP TO SUCCEED

We are a not-for-profit organization sponsored by a community of like-minded individuals. Our content is freely available, fulfilling our basic philosophy of making education accessible to all who wish to enjoy our stories. We welcome and encourage you to contribute to this project and assist us in promoting, developing and producing an array of valuable educational content.

YOU CAN CONTRIBUTE TOO!

  • providing new ideas and initiatives, by submitting your input and feedback
  • growing this community by sharing the content with others
  • translating and localizing the content so as to expand readership to more geographies
  • donations will assist us in continuing to create high quality content for children

"The Voice of Reason" team would love to hear from you. Please contact us to swap ideas and information, provide comments, or for any contributions.
