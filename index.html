ADVENTURES OF GRANDPA BENNY

The Grandpa Benny series of stories are written for children of ages 3-10, with the aim entertaining these youngsters while introducing them to significant achievements in the history of science, exploration and adventure. These books offer a friendly and fun-filled learning environment appropriate for both school and home use, and provide the seeds for inquisitive kids to discover world events.
